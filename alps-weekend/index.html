an easy way into the alps

This is a small, beautifully held weekend for women who want the mountains without the planning spiral.

Yoga, hikes, picnic, lake swimming, transport, tables, views, and the full rhythm are thought through from beginning to end. You arrive, breathe, walk, eat well, and let the landscape do its quiet work.

the stay

a simple stay with an unreal mountain view

Nothing overdone. Just fresh air, wooden balconies, flowers, and a small alpine village where the morning feels almost unreal before the day begins.

what we will move through

A full alpine weekend, held gently: enough adventure to feel alive, enough space to actually land in it.
